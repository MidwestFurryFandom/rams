Judging will begin soon and go until {{ c.MIVS_JUDGING_DEADLINE|datetime_local }}.

As a judge, you'll be responsible for judging around a dozen games. Expect to spend at least 15 minutes per game to get a good idea of how the game feels.

You'll be giving the games a score from 1-10 based on three categories:
  • Show Readiness - how ready the game appears to be shown to the public. The quality of visual and audio assets and the lack of significant game play bugs.
  • Overall Design - how well the game addresses its central concept. (i.e. If it is a puzzle game, are the puzzles good? If it is a narrative-driven game, is the story compelling?)
  • Overall Enjoyment - how enjoyable was the game? Did it keep you on your toes, make you laugh, push your reflexes to the limit?

There will also be a space for you to leave constructive feedback that will be sent to the developers after judging completes.

You will have until {{ c.MIVS_JUDGING_DEADLINE|datetime_local }} to complete judging for your entries.
