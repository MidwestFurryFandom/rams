When the lottery concludes and results are announced, anyone awarded a room must then follow up and post a payment guarantee (credit card) to their reservation once it is created. Award information will include the deadline to post guarantee and instructions on how to do do so.

Hotel policy requires we cancel reservations without a form of payment to guarantee the reservation. Every year we have cancellations for lack of payment guarantee. PLEASE pay attention to your email following the conclusion of the lottery, and ensure you promptly confirm your reservation with a payment guarantee.
